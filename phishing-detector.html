How to Identify Phishing

Check the Sender

Verify the email address or phone number. Be suspicious of slight variations.

Inspect Links

Hover over links to see the real URL. Don't click if it doesn't match the sender.

Watch for Urgency

Be wary of messages creating pressure to act immediately.

Never Share Info

Legitimate companies never ask for passwords or personal data via email/SMS.
